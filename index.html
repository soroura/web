Areas of Work
Four problems I keep coming back to

Emergency Care Systems

WHO frameworks sound clear in Geneva. Getting them to work inside an Egyptian hospital is a different problem entirely — and that's the one I'm focused on.

Disaster Preparedness

Mass casualty drills, hospital resilience, surge planning. The infrastructure nobody thinks about until the day everything depends on it.

Applied Health Technology

If it doesn't work offline, in Arabic, on a phone with 30% battery — it doesn't work in the field. That constraint shapes everything I build.

Health Data Science

What actually happens in emergency departments and disaster responses — not what the reports say happens. Currently finishing an MSc at Edinburgh.
