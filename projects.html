Emergency Simulation Exercise Platform

In Development

A digital tool for structured simulation exercise design, facilitation, and after-action review. Uses AI-assisted scenario generation and inject sequencing to support facility-level exercises in low-resource settings.

Grounded in WHO Simulation Exercise Management methodology. Designed to reduce exercise preparation burden for small teams and reduce reliance on specialist external facilitators, while maintaining structured debriefing and learning capture.

Current Research

MSc Dissertation: TraumaApp ED Data Analysis

In Progress

A work-based dissertation project at the University of Edinburgh, utilising real-life data captured by the TraumaApp at a Scottish hospital during its pilot phase. The TraumaApp captures timed steps of the ED journey in a way that traditional registries do not, making it possible to examine the factors that affect both ED length of stay and time to definitive care with a granularity that conventional data sources cannot easily provide.
